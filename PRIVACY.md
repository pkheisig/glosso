# Privacy Policy for WordPeek

**Last Updated:** January 10, 2026

## Overview

WordPeek is a browser extension that displays dictionary definitions from Wiktionary. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**WordPeek does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

### What we store locally:
- Your selected language preference
- Words you choose to save to your vocabulary list

This data is stored entirely on your device using Chrome's local storage API. It is never transmitted to any server.

### What we access:
- **Wiktionary API (en.wiktionary.org):** We fetch word definitions from Wiktionary's public API. Only the word you look up is sent to Wiktionaryâ€”no other information about you or your browsing activity.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- No personal information
- No browsing history
- No cookies or tracking
- No analytics or telemetry
- No user accounts

## Third-Party Services

The only external service used is Wiktionary (wikimedia.org), which has its own [privacy policy](https://foundation.wikimedia.org/wiki/Privacy_policy).

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
